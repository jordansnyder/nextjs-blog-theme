Jordan is a multi-faceted leader of creative and technical innovators with 25 years of experience in advertising, technology and design consulting, startups, Fortune 500/100 companies and, most recently, art and themed entertainment.

As both a creative and technical person, she finds inspiration at the intersection of technology, art, business, and entrepreneurship. She seeks to inspire and lead teams of diverse and innovative folks to build next-generation transformative experiences, products, and platforms.

When she's not working as a creative technology executive in the film industry, Jordan volunteers as a firefighter, EMT and member of a technical rescue team in Santa Fe, New Mexico, US.
